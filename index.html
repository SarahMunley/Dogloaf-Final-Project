    COMING SOON
"Hey Dad, what's the weather supposed to be like today?"
"Eh...it might rain. Or it might not"

Well, yes - those are the only two possible outcomes. Like those who learned at the feet of Marcus Aurelius, I will remember this stoic lesson for the rest of my life.

You have no control over the weather, but you can control your attitude toward change, adversity and the unexpected. Be ready. Be open.
